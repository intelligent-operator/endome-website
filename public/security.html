How sign-in works

Two-factor by default. Every login sends a 6-digit code to your email. You need both your password and that code, every time.

Sessions last 30 days. Use Sign out from the menu in the top right to end the current session on this device.

Cookies are signed. A tampered session cookie is rejected automatically — you'd be bounced to login.
