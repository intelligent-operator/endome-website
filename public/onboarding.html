About endometriosis

A condition where tissue similar to the uterine lining grows outside the uterus — causing pain, heavy periods, fatigue and (in some cases) fertility challenges. 1 in 9 women live with it, and the average time-to-diagnosis is still ~7 years. EndoMe is built to shorten that.
